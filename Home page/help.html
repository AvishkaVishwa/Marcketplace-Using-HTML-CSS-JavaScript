How to Use Dream House

Welcome to the Dream House help page. Here, you'll find information on how to navigate and use our platform effectively.

Getting Started

To begin using Marketplace, you can start by creating an account and logging in. Once logged in, you'll have access to various features and services.

Exploring Services

Our platform offers services such as finding home furnishings,appliances and exploring interior design ideas. You can navigate to these sections using the navigation menu at the top and the image links. you can customise your needs according to your personal ideas by simply contact us.

Settings

In the settings menu, you can manage your account details, update personal informations(name,address,contact numbers,etc), and access help resources.

Need Further Assistance?

If you require further assistance or have any questions, feel free to contact our support team. We're here to help you make the most out of Marketplace.
